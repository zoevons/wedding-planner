Our wedding planning service provides couples with a truly comprehensive and uniquely tailored experience. We work collaboratively with carefully selected vendors to curate a wedding day which truly reflects your relationship. From the moment we first meet to the very end of your wedding celebrations, we endeavour to ensure your planning journey is memorable, exciting, and representative of who you are as a couple. We'll begin by creating a detailed and bespoke Design Plan before moving on to supervise your budget, select the perfect venue, build the best vendor team, arrange details and logistics, and finally co-ordinate your wedding day itself.

Venues

We have some amazing locations for wedding venues in and around Hertfordshire. Whatever theme or style you choose, we have a perfect location to suit you.

Flowers

From bridal bouquets to button holes, our wedding collections from award winning florists will add a touch of sophistication to your big day.

Wedding Cakes

Our beautiful, unique wedding cakes are elegantly handmade in Hertfordshire. We understand that such an important celebration deserves a truly special cake and that is why we go to great lengths to create the wedding cake of your dreams.

Photography

We work with an extensive list of wedding photographers to capture your perfect day. We will match you the perfect photographer for you based on your requirements, theme and style.

Hair & Make-Up

Look flawless, elegant and timeless on your wedding day with a beautiful, bespoke make-up look and hair style created by a specialist and award winning wedding make up artist and hair stylist.

Transport

Would you like to arrive to your wedding in a vintage VW camper van, a classic Rolls Royce or a double decker bus? What ever vehicle you decide we will get you there in time and in style.

With a wealth of local experience, an eye for detail and a down-to-earth approach, we always excited and honoured to play a key part in such an emotional and immensely important day in two peoples lives.
